How do I download my bank's CSV? ▼
  • Chase: Sign in → Accounts → Download account activity → choose date range → CSV
  • Bank of America: Accounts → Transaction history → Download → Comma delimited text
  • Capital One: Accounts → Transactions → Download transactions → CSV
  • Ally: Account activity → Download (top right) → CSV
  • Wells Fargo: Account summary → Download account activity → Comma-Separated Values
  • Other banks: Look for "Download", "Export", or "Statements" near your transaction history. If unsure, search: [your bank name] export transactions CSV.
